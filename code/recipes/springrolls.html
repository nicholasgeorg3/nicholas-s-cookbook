Vietnamese Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Rice vermicelli
  • Shrimp
  • Lettuce
  • Mint
  • Cilantro
  • Peanut sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles and shrimp
  2. Prepare vegetables
  3. Soften rice paper
  4. Assemble rolls
  5. Make peanut sauce

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate
