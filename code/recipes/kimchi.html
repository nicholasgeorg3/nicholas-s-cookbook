Korean Kimchi

Ingredients

  • Napa cabbage
  • Korean red pepper flakes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Fish sauce
  • Green onions
  • Korean radish
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Salt cabbage
  2. Make pepper paste
  3. Mix vegetables
  4. Combine all ingredients
  5. Ferment for 1-5 days

Preparation Time: 2 hours

Fermentation: 1-5 days

Difficulty: Advanced
