Chinese Dumplings (Jiaozi)

Ingredients

  • Dumpling wrappers
  • Ground pork
  • Napa cabbage
  • Chinese chives
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Mix filling ingredients
  2. Place filling in wrappers
  3. Fold and pleat dumplings
  4. Boil or pan-fry
  5. Serve with dipping sauce

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Servings: 30 dumplings

Difficulty: Advanced
