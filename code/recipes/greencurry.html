Thai Green Curry

Ingredients

  • Green curry paste
  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken
  • Thai eggplants
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Thai basil
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar

Instructions

  1. Fry curry paste
  2. Add coconut milk
  3. Cook chicken
  4. Add vegetables
  5. Season and simmer
  6. Garnish with basil

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate
