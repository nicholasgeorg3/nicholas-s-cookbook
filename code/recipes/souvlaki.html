Greek Souvlaki

Ingredients

  • Pork or chicken
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Pita bread
  • Tzatziki sauce

Instructions

  1. Marinate meat
  2. Thread onto skewers
  3. Grill until charred
  4. Warm pita bread
  5. Serve with tzatziki

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy
