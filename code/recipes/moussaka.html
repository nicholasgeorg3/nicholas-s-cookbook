Greek Moussaka

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Ground lamb
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Greek spices
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Salt and grill eggplant
  2. Make meat sauce
  3. Prepare béchamel
  4. Layer ingredients
  5. Top with béchamel
  6. Bake until golden

Cooking Time: 2 hours

Servings: 8

Difficulty: Advanced
