Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Shrimp or chicken
  • Tofu
  • Bean sprouts
  • Eggs
  • Tamarind paste
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Crushed peanuts

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles
  2. Make sauce
  3. Stir-fry proteins
  4. Add noodles and sauce
  5. Add eggs and sprouts
  6. Garnish and serve

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate
