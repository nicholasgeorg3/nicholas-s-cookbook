Ratatouille

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Yellow squash
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Fresh herbs

Instructions

  1. Slice vegetables thinly
  2. Make tomato sauce base
  3. Arrange vegetables in pattern
  4. Season and drizzle with oil
  5. Bake until tender

Cooking Time: 1.5 hours

Servings: 6

Difficulty: Intermediate
