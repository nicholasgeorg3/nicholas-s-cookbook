Indian Butter Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Yogurt
  • Tomato sauce
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Garam masala
  • Various Indian spices
  • Garlic and ginger

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices
  2. Cook chicken until browned
  3. Make sauce with tomatoes and cream
  4. Add butter and spices
  5. Combine chicken with sauce
  6. Simmer until thick
  7. Serve with naan bread

Cooking Time: 1 hour

Servings: 6

Difficulty: Intermediate
