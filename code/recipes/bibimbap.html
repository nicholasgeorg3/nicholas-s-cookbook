Korean Bibimbap

Ingredients

  • Steamed rice
  • Ground beef
  • Spinach
  • Bean sprouts
  • Carrots
  • Mushrooms
  • Fried egg
  • Gochujang sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Prepare vegetables
  3. Season and cook beef
  4. Fry egg
  5. Assemble in bowl
  6. Add gochujang and mix

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate
