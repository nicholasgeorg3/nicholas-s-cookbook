Chicken Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded chicken
  • Enchilada sauce
  • Shredded cheese
  • Onions
  • Black olives
  • Sour cream

Instructions

  1. Prepare enchilada sauce
  2. Soften tortillas
  3. Fill with chicken and cheese
  4. Roll and arrange in baking dish
  5. Top with sauce and cheese
  6. Bake until bubbly

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 6

Difficulty: Intermediate
