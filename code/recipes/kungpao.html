Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Peanuts
  • Dried red chilies
  • Scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Szechuan peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken
  2. Toast peanuts
  3. Stir-fry chicken
  4. Add sauce and chilies
  5. Combine with peanuts

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate
