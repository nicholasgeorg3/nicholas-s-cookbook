Coq au Vin

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • Red wine
  • Bacon lardons
  • Pearl onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Carrots
  • Fresh herbs
  • Chicken stock

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in wine
  2. Cook bacon and vegetables
  3. Brown chicken
  4. Add wine and stock
  5. Simmer until tender
  6. Reduce sauce

Cooking Time: 2 hours

Servings: 6

Difficulty: Advanced
