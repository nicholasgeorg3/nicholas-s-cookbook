Tonkotsu Ramen

Ingredients

  • Pork bones
  • Ramen noodles
  • Chashu pork
  • Soft-boiled eggs
  • Green onions
  • Nori
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Garlic, ginger

Instructions

  1. Simmer pork bones for 12-18 hours
  2. Prepare toppings (chashu, eggs)
  3. Cook noodles
  4. Strain broth
  5. Assemble bowls with broth and noodles
  6. Add toppings
  7. Serve immediately

Cooking Time: 18 hours

Servings: 6-8

Difficulty: Advanced
