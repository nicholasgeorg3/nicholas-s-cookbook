Mexican Street Tacos

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Carne asada (grilled beef)
  • White onion
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Salsa verde
  • Guacamole

Instructions

  1. Marinate and grill the beef
  2. Chop onions and cilantro
  3. Warm the tortillas
  4. Assemble tacos with meat and toppings
  5. Serve with lime and salsas

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Easy
