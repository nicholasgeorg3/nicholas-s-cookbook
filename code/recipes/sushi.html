Homemade Sushi Rolls

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • Nori sheets
  • Rice vinegar
  • Fresh fish (salmon, tuna)
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Wasabi
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook sushi rice and season with vinegar
  2. Let rice cool to room temperature
  3. Place nori on bamboo mat
  4. Spread rice on nori
  5. Add fillings
  6. Roll tightly using mat
  7. Cut into pieces with sharp knife

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4-6

Difficulty: Intermediate
