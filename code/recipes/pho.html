Vietnamese Pho

Ingredients

  • Beef bones
  • Rice noodles
  • Beef slices
  • Bean sprouts
  • Thai basil
  • Star anise
  • Cinnamon
  • Fish sauce

Instructions

  1. Make bone broth
  2. Cook rice noodles
  3. Prepare garnishes
  4. Assemble bowls
  5. Add hot broth
  6. Serve with condiments

Cooking Time: 6-8 hours

Servings: 6

Difficulty: Advanced
